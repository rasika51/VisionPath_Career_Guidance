DEGREE SELECTOR

Thinking of studying at a local university? Use our interactive tool below to find degree courses you can do based on your A/L subject stream and your preferred university. It is important to understand the potential options available to you based on your selected A/L subject stream.

The Degree Selector tool is simply an aid to understand potential degrees for different A/L subject streams based on 2018/2019 UGC entrance requirements. The University Grants Commission (UGC) has specific entry requirements for every admission year. You are advised to refer to the relevant UGC handbook of your admission year to find exact requirements. Do not make A/L subject choices based solely on this tool.
